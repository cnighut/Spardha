ABOUT


SPARDHA is an All India Aptitude-cum-Talent Search Examination conducted under 'Prayatna',
the social drive of Alma Fiesta, the Annual Socio-Cultural fest of IIT Bhubaneswar.
It aims to inculcate among students, a sense of general awareness towards their social responsibility.

NATIONAL PLATFORM

SPARDHA provides a unique platform for students to compete against the brightest and innovative minds over the country and apply their knowledge and reasoning to shine among the crowd.

INNOVATIVE APPROACH

SPARDHA aims to create an awareness about the importance of general awareness along with reviving the lost curiosity and interest among the students by providing them with a test that challenges their analytical thinking.

PRAYATNA

This year “Prayatna" - the social campaign associated with ALMA FIESTA had taken up the issue of blood donation with a theme of "Share Life: The blood donation drive."

PRIZES

CERTIFICATION

Each participating student will recieve a certificate of participation from Alma-Fiesta, IIT Bhubaneswar.

ANALYSIS REPORT

Each participating student will recieve a detailed analysis report of their performance in SPARDHA and the report will be strictly confined to the student's personal performance.

SCHOOL AND REGIONAL PERFORMANCE

A special certificate of recognition will be given to distinctive performers of each school and/or respective city/region.

INTERVIEW

The short-listed students will have an exclusive interview published on the website by Alma Fiesta, IIT Bhubaneswar.
